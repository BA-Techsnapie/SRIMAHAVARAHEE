Sacred Timeline

1847

Foundation of Faith

The sacred foundation stone was laid by revered Saint Maharaj Gurudev, following divine visions that guided him to this blessed location. The temple's construction began with traditional Vastu Shastra principles, ensuring perfect harmony between earthly and celestial energies.

Sacred Foundation Ceremony
1852

Architectural Marvel

Master craftsmen from across India collaborated to create the temple's intricate architecture. The main sanctum features traditional Dravidian style with elaborate carvings depicting divine stories, while the prayer halls showcase perfect acoustic design for chanting and devotional music.

Sacred Geometry Implementation
1855

Divine Consecration

The temple was consecrated with elaborate Vedic rituals spanning 21 days, attended by thousands of devotees. The main deity was installed with proper Prana Pratishtha ceremony, infusing divine consciousness into the sacred murti and establishing the temple as a living spiritual entity.
